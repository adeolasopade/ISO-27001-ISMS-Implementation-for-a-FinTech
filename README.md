# ISO 27001 ISMS Implementation for Cbet Limited (FinTech)
*This project draws on real consulting work, with all company details fully anonymised.*

---

Led the design and implementation of an ISO 27001 [ISMS](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1VG4eUwdDHJRyrh02FRi145ps_a1IzUXv?usp=sharing) for a Nigerian payment service provider, securing payment systems, aligning with PCI DSS and CBN regulations, and establishing a scalable, trust-based foundation for growth.

---

## The Approach

*   **Context & Scope**: [Defined](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1nrKtFWrWeb6MkO5cZFXxUo9ts5XAZMef/edit?usp=sharing&ouid=101134501969411208830&rtpof=true&sd=true) business environment, regulatory obligations, and a moderate risk appetite.

*   **Governance**: [Used](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1AvTCrkFjSFStlr2iJxSXdLoR3xeU_E-e/edit?usp=drive_link&ouid=101134501969411208830&rtpof=true&sd=true) a RACI model to assign accountability, designating the CTO as the operational security owner.

*   **Operational Processes**: Established procedures for [communication](https://docs.google.com/document/d/17R6p5W4crq-N-yEIsoUd8mFtibFp-Njv/edit?usp=drive_link&ouid=101134501969411208830&rtpof=true&sd=true), document [control](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1d2cNl19gGzUB8nAXf5qy9trKePsh_Mp_/edit?usp=drive_link&ouid=101134501969411208830&rtpof=true&sd=true), and risk [assessment](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1a5zqlg7k_eBCS7S2nK2ZMxGQHscExWCe/edit?usp=drive_link&ouid=101134501969411208830&rtpof=true&sd=true) using a 3x3 matrix.

*   **Assurance**: Closed the PDCA loop with an internal audit [program](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1ZngT2EKaHxATvBoAt_ltlkOiR8fCIYD4/edit?usp=drive_link&ouid=101134501969411208830&rtpof=true&sd=true) and a metrics [calendar](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1gPHaKMgAb-QoRcj3msIq_4B8UI3985Z4/edit?usp=drive_link&ouid=101134501969411208830&rtpof=true&sd=true) to track KPIs and drive improvement.

---

## Practical Insights & Recommendations

*   **Incomplete Risk Treatments**: Vendor delays slowed remediation, as flagged in the Metrics Calendar. Highlighted need for stronger vendor management.

*   **Security Awareness Gaps**: New hires and shift staff missed training, underscoring need for onboarding-integrated sessions.

*   **Procedural Lapses**: Controls like the emergency contact list were informal, validating the need for enforced document [control](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1d2cNl19gGzUB8nAXf5qy9trKePsh_Mp_/edit?usp=drive_link&ouid=101134501969411208830&rtpof=true&sd=true).

---

## Reflection & Learning

The project confirmed an effective ISMS is an operational enabler. Clear ownership, repeatable processes, and measurable outcomes create a system that finds and fixes its own gaps.
